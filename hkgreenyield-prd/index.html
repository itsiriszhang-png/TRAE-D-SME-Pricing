1.2 Three Core Pain Points

Pain Point 1: Investment Barrier — Retail Excluded
HSBC Orion and DigiFT minimum subscription: USD 5,000–10,000. Institutional products: USD 100,000+. ~60% of HK retail investors have monthly savings of HKD 5,000–15,000 — effectively excluded from green finance entirely.
Pain Point 2: Liquidity Trap — Can't Exit
Traditional green bonds settle T+2 with high friction, locking retail holders for 5+ years. Liquidity premium erosion means actual returns are significantly lower than stated yield.
Pain Point 3: Greenwashing — No Independent Verification
EU SFDR investigations and HK SFC 2025 guidelines highlight systemic greenwashing. Retail investors have no way to independently verify whether their "green" investment is actually reducing emissions.

3.3 Core User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

Epic 1 — Account Registration & KYC
"As a Hong Kong retail investor, I want to complete account registration and identity verification within 5 minutes, so that I can immediately start buying green bond tokens without being deterred by a lengthy process."
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Face recognition completes in < 60 seconds
  • KYC result returned in real-time (AI initial review + human fallback)
  • On rejection: clear reason + appeal entry point provided
  • ERC-3643 whitelist auto-updated; zero manual intervention
Epic 2 — First Token Purchase
"As a KYC-verified retail user, I want to purchase a specific green project's token for HKD 200, so that I can participate in investment and start tracking my environmental impact."
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Project listing shows APY / VCS certification / remaining quota
  • Purchase completes in ≤ 3 steps (select → input amount → confirm)
  • Gas fee < HKD 0.10 per transaction (Polygon L2)
  • Tokens appear in account within 30 seconds of purchase confirmation
  • Supports FPS and Faster Payment System top-up
Epic 3 — ESG Impact Tracking
"As a token holder, I want to view real-time CO₂ reduction data corresponding to my investment, so that I feel the environmental value of my investment and can share it with friends."
Acceptance Criteria:
  • Dashboard data updated hourly (triggered by Chainlink Oracle)
  • Displays: personal CO₂ reduction (cumulative / this month) + equivalence ("equivalent to planting X trees")
  • Supports generating shareable card (with NFT on-chain verification code)
  • Data source link publicly accessible and on-chain verifiable

Compliance & Regulatory Framework
Regulatory Positioning
HKGreenYield will enter the market via HKMA Project Ensemble Sandbox, which provides a structured regulatory testing environment. Year-2 target: apply for SFC Type 1 (Dealing in Securities) licence to support full retail distribution.

5.1 KYC / AML Framework

JurisdictionKYC RequirementOur Implementation
Hong KongHKMA CDD guidelines; SFC AML noticeAI face ID + HK ID/passport OCR; ERC-3643 on-chain whitelist; auto-freeze on suspicious activity
Mainland ChinaCross-border investment restrictions; SAFE complianceGBA users via licensed Hong Kong entity only; no direct CNY on-ramp in Phase 1
InstitutionalKYB (Know Your Business) + UBO disclosureManual enhanced due diligence for tickets >USD 10,000; compliance officer sign-off

5.2 Token Classification

  • HKGY tokens are classified as security tokens (representing fractional ownership in green bonds) under SFC's regulatory framework — not utility tokens or payment tokens
  • ERC-3643 standard legally enforces transfer restrictions: tokens can only be transferred between KYC-verified addresses, satisfying HKMA's investor protection requirements
  • Carbon credit tokens may qualify as environmental instruments under SFC's 2025 green product guidelines — providing potential regulatory fast-track

5.3 Data Privacy & Storage

  • Personal KYC data stored off-chain in Hong Kong-domiciled servers (compliant with PDPO)
  • On-chain data limited to: wallet address, token balance, ESG attribution, transaction history — zero PII on-chain
  • ONCHAINID (ERC-3643 identity module) stores cryptographic proof only — not actual identity documents
